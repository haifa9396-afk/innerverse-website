Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Innerverse is a self-discovery platform built on the belief that you deserve to be truly known. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Innerverse mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile data — your MBTI type, Enneagram type, wound type, and zodiac sign as determined through our assessments
  • Journal entries — personal reflections and writing you submit within the app
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, session frequency, and feature usage
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and app version for technical support

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to power and improve your Innerverse experience:

  • To generate your personalised psychological profile
  • To provide contextually aware AI reflection responses through our Reflect feature
  • To store and display your journal entries and Soul Notes
  • To manage your subscription via RevenueCat
  • To improve app performance and fix issues

AI and Your Data

Innerverse uses the Anthropic Claude API to power the Reflect feature. Your psychological profile and journal entries are sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalised responses. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not use your personal data to train AI models.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate Innerverse:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI reflection processing
  • RevenueCat — subscription management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Innerverse is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete their data immediately.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure cloud storage, and server-side API key management. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Innerverse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
