Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Innerverse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Innerverse mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (email address, name)
  • Personality quiz responses and results
  • Journal entries and reflection session content
  • In-app purchase history via RevenueCat

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Innerverse app
  • Generate your personalised personality profile
  • Power the AI reflection companion using your profile context
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you app notifications if you have opted in

AI & Data Processing

Innerverse uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power the reflection companion. Your journal entries and profile data are processed through a secure server-side proxy — your data is never sent directly to third-party AI providers from your device. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Journal entries and profile data are stored in your personal account and are not shared with other users.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • Anthropic — AI language model (server-side only)
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery infrastructure

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request data deletion, contact us at the email below. Account deletion removes all associated data from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Innerverse is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
